The Complete Benchmark

MARC expands on the ARC challenge with multimodal and reasoning-heavy tasks, creating the most comprehensive general intelligence benchmark to date.

Core ARC-Style Tasks

40 puzzles

  • Pattern recognition and transformations
  • Grid-based reasoning challenges
  • Abstraction without domain-specific hacks
  • Novel problem settings to prevent memorization

Math & Logical Reasoning

10 word problems

  • Algebra and probability challenges
  • Logic puzzles requiring deep reasoning
  • Numerical reasoning tasks
  • Novel contexts to prevent pattern memorization

Audio Understanding

10 audio challenges

  • Spoken instruction comprehension
  • Overlapping sounds analysis
  • Rhythm and melody pattern matching
  • Environmental sound reasoning

Visual Reasoning

10 image tasks

  • Odd-object identification
  • Visual transformation analysis
  • Pattern recognition in sequences
  • Spatial reasoning challenges

Retrieval-Augmented Generation

10 RAG questions

  • Information retrieval from provided corpus
  • Combining retrieval with reasoning
  • Comparative analysis tasks
  • Multi-document synthesis

Video Understanding

10 video clips

  • Next frame/event prediction
  • Temporal sequence reasoning
  • Causal relationship understanding
  • Dynamic scene analysis

Why MARC Will Become the Standard

🎯 Multimodal & Multi-Reasoning

Unlike ARC alone, MARC tests across all core modalities that general AI must handle

🚫 Prevents Gaming

Forces models to generalize across modalities instead of becoming benchmark-specialists

🎨 Comprehensive Coverage

Tests abstraction, math, language, vision, audio, retrieval, and video understanding

⚖️ Fair & Balanced

Each subtask contributes equally with partial credit for reasoning tasks
